Privacy Policy for NettalkEmailOauth application

Application home page: https://github.com/oleg-fomin/NettalkEmailOAuth

Effective Date: 1/1/2025

Thank you for using our NettalkEmailOauth application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our application, especially in relation to Google OAuth.

Information We Collect

When you use Google OAuth to sign in to NettalkEmailOauth, we may collect the following information from your Google account:
  • Basic Profile Information: This typically includes your name, email address, profile picture, and language preference. We only collect the minimum necessary information required for user authentication and personalization within our app. Specifically, we request access to gmail.send Google scope only. We do not request access to any sensitive scopes that are not strictly required for our app's functionality.
  • Access token: Google OAuth access token, refresh token, token expiration date and time will be collected and stored in the application folder oAuth.ini file on your hard drive. You can find and clear this informayion at any time.
We do not collect any other information from your Google account without your explicit consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • Core functionality: ability to send Email using modern authentication protocol (Google OAuth).
  • Communication: We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, or service-related announcements. You can manage your communication preferences in your account settings.
  • Improving Our Services: We may use aggregated and anonymized data to analyze how our app is used and to identify areas for improvement.

Information Sharing

We will not share your personal information with any third parties except in the following limited circumstances:
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our app (e.g., hosting, analytics). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
  • We do not have access to the application folder on your hard drive, and You take your own responsibility for OAuth access token stored in the oAuth.ini file after You granted requested access to our application.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. You can delete your account at any time, which will also delete your personal information from our systems, except as required by law.

Here’s how to easily revoke access you previously granted to our application:

  • Head to Your Google Account: Open your web browser and visit https://myaccount.google.com.
  • Security Settings: Click on “Security” from the left-hand menu.
  • Manage App Access: Scroll down to the “Third-party apps with account access” section and click “Manage third-party access.”
  • Find the App: Locate the app whose access you want to revoke.
  • Revoke Access: Click on the app and choose “Remove Access.”

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage some of your information directly within your Google account settings. For other requests, please contact us at "Oleg Fomin" oleg@fomin.info.
